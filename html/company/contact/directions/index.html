From Portland Airport

The following directions will take you from Portland International Airport to our corporate headquarters in Lake Oswego.

  • Take Airport Way to I-205 South (approx. 1.9 miles)
  • Merge onto I-205 S / VETERANS MEMORIAL HWY toward I-84 / PORTLAND / SALEM (3.0 miles)
  • Merge onto I-84 W / US-30 W via EXIT 21B toward PORTLAND (5.4 miles)
  • Merge onto I-5 S via the exit on the LEFT toward BEAVERTON / SALEM (9.2 miles)
  • Take the OR-217 / KRUSE WAY exit (EXIT 292) toward LAKE OSWEGO (0.3 miles)
  • Turn LEFT onto KRUSE WAY (1.1 miles)
  • Take the first right after crossing Carman Drive to enter Kruse Way Plaza
  • Vencore Capital is located in Building I / 4500
